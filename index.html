About A Child's Place

Nurturing Young Minds Since 1987

At A Child's Place, we believe that every child deserves a loving, safe, and stimulating environment to grow and learn. Our daycare has been a cornerstone of the community for over a decade, providing exceptional childcare services that support both children and families.

We understand that choosing the right daycare is one of the most important decisions you'll make as a parent. That's why we've created a warm, welcoming space where children feel secure, valued, and excited to learn every day.

Our Core Services

Comprehensive care programs designed for every stage of your child's development

Infant Care

Tender loving care for babies aged 6 weeks to 18 months. Our infant program focuses on creating secure attachments and meeting each baby's individual needs.

  • Individualized feeding schedules
  • Safe sleep environments
  • Sensory play activities
  • Daily communication with parents

Toddler Care

Active learning environment for toddlers aged 18 months to 3 years. We encourage exploration, independence, and social skill development.

  • Structured play activities
  • Potty training support
  • Language development
  • Social interaction skills

Preschool Programs

School readiness program for children aged 3-5 years. Our curriculum prepares children for kindergarten through hands-on learning experiences.

  • Early literacy & numeracy
  • Creative arts & crafts
  • Science exploration
  • Kindergarten preparation

Summer Programs

Fun-filled summer activities including outdoor play, water activities, field trips, and special themed weeks to keep learning exciting.

  • Outdoor adventures
  • Water play activities
  • Educational field trips
  • Themed activity weeks

Extended Hours

Flexible scheduling options to accommodate working parents, including early drop-off and late pick-up services.

  • Early morning drop-off
  • Late evening pick-up
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Emergency care options

Nutritious Meals

Healthy, balanced meals and snacks prepared fresh daily, accommodating dietary restrictions and promoting good eating habits.

  • Fresh, healthy meals
  • Dietary accommodations
  • Nutrition education
  • Allergy-conscious menu

Why Choose A Child's Place?

Discover what makes our daycare the perfect choice for your family

Safety First

Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art security systems, childproof environments, and strict safety protocols to ensure your child's wellbeing.

Experienced Staff

Our team consists of qualified early childhood educators with years of experience and ongoing professional development training.

Educational Activities

Age-appropriate curriculum that promotes cognitive, social, emotional, and physical development through play-based learning.

Small Class Sizes

Low teacher-to-child ratios ensure personalized attention and individualized care for each child's unique needs.

Home-Like Environment

Warm, comfortable spaces designed to feel like a home away from home, promoting emotional security and comfort.

Open Communication

Regular updates, daily reports, and open-door policy to keep parents informed and involved in their child's development.
